Love to the Grave (Amharic: ፍቅር እስከ መቃብር) is an Amharic novel by
Haddis Alemayehu published in 1968. It is one of the best known novels in
Ethiopia and is considered a classic of Ethiopian literature.
The novel gained popularity largely due to its widespread dissemination on Ethiopian radio during the Derg regime.
